About Me

Hello, I am Jayant!

I am originally hailing from Mumbai, India, and making the United States my home since the age of two. While some might find their strengths in computer science and math, my academic journey has led me to discover a deep passion for history and English. Exploring the intricate stories woven into the fabric of our past and expressing myself through language are where I truly thrive. Currently pursuing a degree in International Relations at George Washington University, I am wholeheartedly dedicated to understanding global dynamics and fostering connections between nations. My academic pursuits revolve around unraveling the complexities of international relations, delving into the nuances of diplomatic discourse, and exploring how history and language shape our global landscape. In addition to my academic pursuits, I find joy in connecting with diverse perspectives, seeking to contribute to a world where understanding and cooperation transcend borders. Join me on this journey as we navigate the complexities of international relations, one historical narrative and linguistic expression at a time.
